ABOUT US

The Best Holidays Start Here!

An historic building at the heart of Farnworth village. Join us here at the Ring O'Bells in our cosy family run, country pub and indulge in a fabulous selection of food and beverage. Why not relax in our ambient bar, from premium wine, local cask ale to continental lager, we've got you covered! Live music every Friday & Saturday, along with our popular jazz Sundays. All your favourite sports ... Even a dog friendly area for your furry friends. And who could forget our delicious bar snack menu. It really is the hub of the community! Fancy dining with us? A seasonal menu created to the flavour profile of the time of year, accompanied by your favourite pub classics! We can also accommodate any social gathering from Christenings to baby showers, engagements or anniversaries, birthdays and wakes, with our function room and catering for up to 50 people. If that's not enough why not enjoy a refreshing beer or cocktail in our huge beer garden during those hot summer months, also featuring a children's play area. There's no better way to keep our community alive and together! So what are you waiting for come and join us here at the Ring O' Bells See you all soon!
